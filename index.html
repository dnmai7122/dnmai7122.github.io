About me

I’m a Data Science student at VNUHCM – University of Science, passionate about working with data and currently pursuing a career path that combines both Data Science and Data Engineering. I enjoy exploring datasets, uncovering patterns, and transforming complex data into meaningful insights that can solve real-world problems and drive innovation.

My work involves applying statistical analysis, machine learning, and engineering principles to design scalable, efficient, and reliable data solutions. I aim to not only build models that deliver accurate predictions but also design robust data pipelines and architectures that ensure data quality, accessibility, and performance. I’m constantly experimenting with new tools and technologies, from Python, R, and SQL to cloud platforms and big data frameworks.

Beyond technical skills, I value clear communication and collaboration. I believe that the most impactful solutions emerge when analytical rigor meets creativity and practical application. My ultimate goal is to contribute to both industry and academia, bridging the gap between data research and data-driven decision-making to create solutions that are not only functional but also sustainable and impactful.
